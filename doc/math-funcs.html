Algebra and Trigonometry Functions

A variety of commonly used math functions are available. They all operate on FLOAT values.

Syntax

ABS(x)
ACOS(x)
ASIN(x)
ATAN(x)
ATAN2(x, y)
CEILING(x)
COS(x)
COSH(x)
EXP(x)
FLOOR(x)
LOG(x)
LOG10(x)
POW(x, y)
ROUND(x)
SIGN(x)
SIN(x)
SINH(x)
SQRT(x)
TAN(x)
TANH(x)

Example

PRINT POW(2, 5);  -- "32"
PRINT SIN(3.14 / 2);  -- "0.9999996829318346"
PRINT CEILING(-1.6);  -- "-1"
PRINT ROUND(5.5);  -- "6"
