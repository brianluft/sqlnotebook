BREAK Statement

Terminates the enclosing WHILE or FOR loop. It is an error to call BREAK outside of a WHILE or FOR loop.

Syntax

BREAK

Examples

  • DECLARE @counter = 1;
    WHILE 1 BEGIN
        PRINT @counter;
        SET @counter = @counter + 1;
        IF @counter > 10
            BREAK;
    END
    Prints the numbers 1 to 10 in increasing order.
